# CorpBank — Frequently Asked Questions

## Loans

**Q: How long does loan approval take?**
Loans up to $500 are approved instantly. Loans above $500 require human review, which typically takes 1–2 business days. If you escalate to a human agent, review can happen in real time during the chat.

**Q: Can I apply for more than one loan at the same time?**
No. You must repay or have your current pending loan resolved before applying for a new one.

**Q: What happens if my loan is rejected?**
You will be notified in the chat. You may reapply after 30 days, or speak to a human agent to discuss alternatives.

**Q: Is there a minimum loan amount?**
The minimum loan amount is $50.

## Bills

**Q: What happens if I miss a payment?**
A late fee of $25 is applied. If a bill remains unpaid for more than 60 days, it may be sent to collections and affect your credit standing at CorpBank.

**Q: Can I pay a bill through this chat?**
You can ask the AI agent to mark a bill as paid once payment has been made externally. The agent cannot process payments directly.

**Q: How far in advance are bills generated?**
Bills are generated 30 days before the due date and appear in your account immediately.

## Accounts

**Q: How many accounts can I have?**
You can hold up to one checking account, one savings account, and one credit account simultaneously.

**Q: Is my balance information real-time?**
Yes. The AI agent queries the live database every time you ask about your balance.

**Q: What currencies are supported?**
All accounts are denominated in USD.

## Security & Identity

**Q: How does the AI agent verify my identity?**
The agent identifies you by your full name and phone number. This is sufficient for informational queries. For actions that modify your account (loans, payments), the agent applies additional business rules.

**Q: Can someone else ask about my account?**
No. Each chat session is tied to a single verified customer. The session cannot be switched to a different customer without starting a new session.

**Q: What data does the AI agent have access to?**
The agent can read your profile, account balances, transaction history, bills, and loan history. It cannot access passwords, PINs, or any external financial data.

## Human Agents

**Q: When should I speak to a human agent?**
- Loan requests above your credit limit
- Credit limit increase requests
- Disputes or complaints
- Any situation where the AI agent says it cannot help

**Q: How long does it take to connect to a human agent?**
During workshop hours, a human agent is available immediately. Outside of support hours, a ticket is created and a human agent will follow up.
