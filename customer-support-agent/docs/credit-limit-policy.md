# CorpBank — Credit Limit Policy

## Default credit limits by account type

| Account Type | Default Credit Limit |
|---|---|
| New customer (< 6 months) | $300 |
| Standard customer | $500 |
| Premium customer | $2,000 |
| VIP customer | $10,000 |

## How to request a credit limit increase

Customers may request a credit limit increase by contacting a human agent. The agent will evaluate:

1. **Payment history**: no overdue bills in the last 6 months.
2. **Account tenure**: minimum 3 months as an active customer.
3. **Income verification**: may be requested for increases above $5,000.
4. **Current utilization**: credit utilization below 80%.

A credit limit increase request cannot be processed by the AI agent — it must be handled by a human agent.

## Automatic reviews

CorpBank performs an automatic credit limit review every 12 months. Customers may receive a limit increase automatically if they meet all of the following:

- No overdue bills in the last 12 months
- No missed loan repayments
- Account active for more than 1 year

## Temporary credit limit

In exceptional circumstances, a human agent may grant a temporary credit limit increase for a specific loan. This does not permanently change the customer's credit limit.

## Decrease of credit limit

CorpBank reserves the right to decrease a customer's credit limit if:

- The customer has 2 or more overdue bills
- A loan repayment has been missed for more than 30 days
