# CorpBank — Products & Services

## Accounts

### Checking Account
- No monthly fee
- Unlimited transactions
- Debit card included
- Overdraft protection up to $100 (no fee)

### Savings Account
- 2.5% annual interest rate
- Minimum balance: $0
- Up to 6 free withdrawals per month; $5 fee per additional withdrawal
- Interest calculated daily, paid monthly

### Credit Account
- Revolving credit line
- Interest rate: 18.9% APR on outstanding balance
- No annual fee
- Minimum payment: 5% of outstanding balance or $25, whichever is greater
